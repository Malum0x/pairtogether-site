Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12 May 2026

What we collect

  • Your account info: email address, and the name + avatar color you set during onboarding. Email is provided by Apple (via Sign in with Apple) or by you (via password sign-in).
  • Tasks, polls, votes, and completions you create within the app.
  • Your space membership: which shared spaces you belong to.

How we use it

We use this data only to make PairTogether work — to keep your tasks synced across your devices, to share them with the people you've added to your space, and to send the occasional sign-in or invitation email.

We do not sell your data. We do not advertise. We do not run analytics that follow you across other apps or websites. We do not share your data with anyone outside the services listed below.

Who we share it with (data processors)

  • Supabase (database hosting, EU region): stores your account and task data. supabase.com/privacy
  • Resend (email delivery): sends authentication and invitation emails on our behalf. resend.com/legal/privacy-policy
  • Apple (Sign in with Apple): provides your identity if you sign in with Apple. apple.com/legal/privacy

What you control

  • View, edit, and delete your tasks and polls anytime in the app.
  • Leave a shared space anytime (Settings → Leave space).
  • Delete your account and wipe all your data anytime (Settings → Reset Everything). This is permanent.
  • Disable notifications anytime in iOS Settings or in-app.

Data retention

Your data lives as long as your account exists. When you delete your account, all your data is removed from our database, including tasks, polls, completions, and any spaces you owned. Spaces you joined as a member remain — your name is removed from them.

Your rights (GDPR/CCPA)

You can request a copy of your data, correction of inaccuracies, or deletion at any time. Email bartoszpanekeu@gmail.com with your request and we'll respond within 30 days.

Changes to this policy

If we change anything material, we'll update the date above and notify you in the app the next time you open it.
